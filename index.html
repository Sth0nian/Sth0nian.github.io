From small businesses to large corporations.
This is sustainable & scalable.

Single shop websites to global corporate platforms, IAC is the only sane way
to "do infrastructure". Being able to deploy, scale, test, redeploy and repeat
endlessly will allow you to make quicker smarter decisions, with the ability to
roll back quickly when you need to or even if you "dont like the new layout".

  • Get a Request

    New features need adding? Fork your infrastructure into a test environment. With one click.

  • Test, break, repeat

    Make smaller more frequent changes. Test. Break. Rebuild. Repeat. Until your code/infra is robust.

  • Push your changes

    Like git, for infrastructure, your entire setup is now version controlled. Happy with testing? Push to staging then production.

  • Daydream / Drink Coffee

    With all that extra time, you better start thinking of what else you can be doing.
